REFER A FRIEND

How it works.

You can invite up to ten friends and earn up to 300 free credits when they spend $10 within 30 days of signup.

You invite

Share a referral link with a friend who isn't a member of Curator Club.

They join

Have them sign up and create their profile in just two simple steps.

Earn Reward

When they spend at least $10 (within 30 days of signup), you can earn 300 free credits.

  • Artists will get 30 free credits for referring another artist to sign up (after they spend $10 on credits).
  • Curators will get $10 for signing up an artist (after they spend $10 on credits).
  • Curators will get $20 for signing up another curator.
  • Curators will get a 50% discount when submitting their own music.

Terms & Conditions

Eligibility:

The referral program ("Program") is open to all active Curator Club members aged 18 and above.You must have an active Curator Club account to participate in the Program.

Qualifying Referrals:

To qualify for rewards, referred friends must create a profile on Curator Club and make a purchase of at least $5 within 30 days of signing up. Only new Curator Club members are eligible as referrals. Existing members cannot be referred.

Timing:

Referral rewards will be processed within 7-10 business days after a successful referral's qualifying purchase.

Modification or Termination:

Curator Club may modify, suspend, or terminate the Program at any time without prior notice.

Compliance:

Participants must comply with all applicable laws and regulations while participating in the Program.

Acceptance of Terms:

Participation in the Program constitutes acceptance of these terms and conditions.

Referral Link:

Participants will receive a unique referral link that can be shared with friends and family. Referrals must sign up for a Curator Club account using the provided referral link.

Rewards:

For each qualified referral, both the referrer and the referred friend will receive $10 cash back (1,000 points).Referrers can earn rewards for up to ten successful referrals, totaling a maximum of $100 cash back (10,000 points). Rewards will be credited as points to your Curator Club account and can be redeemed for cash back or other available rewards.

Fraudulent Activity:

Curator Club reserves the right to void rewards and terminate a participant's account if fraudulent activity, including but not limited to, self-referrals or deceptive practices, is detected.

Privacy:

By participating in the Program, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Contact:

For questions or concerns related to the Program, please contact our customer support.
